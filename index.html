Developement of object detection, recognition and tracking process on aerial images (UAV) utilizing neural networks

In this master thesis project I examined the feasibility of technical neural networks, in particular by applying convolutional neural networks (CNN’s), to locate, identify and track objects through aerial images taken by unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs). The objects identified and tracked herein are vehicles. In addition, an attempt is made to create a holistic integrated application that performs the above in real-time via live video streaming from UAV. This application is proving to be particularly useful in various fields, both civil and military, such as security, policing, transportation, road planning, search and rescue.
